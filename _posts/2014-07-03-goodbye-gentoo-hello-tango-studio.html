--- layout: post title: Goodbye Gentoo, Hello Tango Studio date: '2014-07-03T07:44:00.000-07:00' author: "@eamoc" tags: modified_time: '2019-01-28T03:16:37.000-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-6955428370236550107.post-9120797444548619759 blogger_orig_url: https://eamoc.blogspot.com/2014/07/goodbye-gentoo-hello-tango-studio.html --- Yep, I finally said farewell to my beloved Gentoo. i really enjoyed working with it, and the Portage System. I was all set to dive in and explore Linux at a lower level, but configuring it for audio/studio recording was just too much of a headache. Jack2 - PulseAudio, its a mess to configure.
So I made the jump to Tango Studio. I have used Ubuntu before, and Ubuntu Studio, but I'm just not that fond of it, it kind of breaks the open source philosophy.
Tango is based on Debian, which ios a bit more "pure".
I'll report on its uses in due course!
